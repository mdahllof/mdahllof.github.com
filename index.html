The aim of RQ1 is to compare a corpus of bestsellers to a corresponding corpus of non-bestsellers concerning three aspects: stylistic, thematic and narrative. The stylistic analysis departs from basic linguistic properties of the text and explores how they distribute, e.g. syntactic structure, inflection (e.g. tense), distribution of form words, vocabulary richness and sentence length (Drożdż et al. 2016). These properties can be determined by means of standard natural language processing (NLP) tools for tagging and parsing.
